Freshly Mart V5.8 - Payment & Receipt Automation
=================================================

Payment rule
------------
All customer payments must be made only to Freshly Mart UPI / QR.
No cash collection by hubs, sellers or delivery partners.

Customer-facing payment modes
-----------------------------
1. UPI Online
2. UPI on Delivery
3. UPI at Hub Pickup

Setup steps
-----------
1. Replace the Apps Script with FreshlyMart_AppsScript.gs.
2. Run setupFreshlyMart().
3. Reload the Google Sheet.
4. Update Settings sheet:
   - UPIID = your Freshly Mart UPI ID
   - UPIQRCodeImageURL = your QR image URL, optional
   - AllowCashPayment = No
5. Deploy Apps Script as Web App again.
6. Paste new Web App URL in assets/app.js.

Receipt workflow
----------------
1. Customer places order.
2. Payment row is created with pending UPI status.
3. Admin verifies UPI payment by OrderID and UPI reference.
4. Backend updates Orders and Payments as Paid to Freshly Mart.
5. Customer receipt is created automatically in Receipts sheet.
6. Paid delivered orders are used for seller/supplier/hub/delivery settlements.

Settlement workflow
-------------------
Generate All Payment Automation creates settlement rows for the current month.
After paying a seller/supplier/hub/delivery partner, use Mark Settlement Paid by ID.
A payment voucher is generated automatically in Receipts and SettlementLog.

Tax summary note
----------------
TaxSummary is a placeholder sheet for CA/GST review. Enter GST/TCS/TDS details as applicable.
