Freshly Mart V5.7 - UPI Only Payment Setup

Final payment rule:
- All customer payments must be made only to Freshly Mart UPI / QR code.
- No cash payment.
- No seller, hub partner or delivery partner should collect cash.
- Hub partners only verify UPI payment proof/reference and hand over the order after payment is done.

Allowed website payment options:
1. UPI Online
   Customer pays immediately after placing the order. Status starts as UPI Pending Verification.

2. UPI on Delivery
   Customer pays at delivery by scanning Freshly Mart UPI QR. Status starts as UPI Pending at Delivery.

3. UPI at Hub Pickup
   Customer pays at the hub by scanning Freshly Mart UPI QR. Status starts as UPI Pending at Hub.

Backend setup:
1. Replace FreshlyMart_AppsScript.gs in Apps Script.
2. Run setupFreshlyMart().
3. Reload the Google Sheet.
4. Enter your UPI ID in Settings sheet row SettingName = UPIID.
5. Optional: add QR image URL in UPIQRCodeImageURL.
6. Redeploy Apps Script as Web App.
7. Paste Web App URL into assets/app.js BACKEND_URL.

Important:
- Old COD rows should not be used for live operations.
- New orders will be blocked if any payment mode outside UPI Online, UPI on Delivery, or UPI at Hub Pickup is submitted.
- Delivery sheets now show: NO CASH. Customer must pay only to Freshly Mart UPI / QR before handover.
